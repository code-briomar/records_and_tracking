Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Records & Tracking ("the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

The app may request access to your device's camera to allow you to capture photos or scan documents as part of its core functionality. No photos or videos are collected or transmitted without your explicit action.

All data captured using the camera remains on your device unless you choose to share or export it. The app does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

2. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device. We do not access, share, or sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (such as Google Firebase) for analytics or notifications. These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the project repository or app support email.
